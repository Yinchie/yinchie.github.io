About Yee Chie and his website.

His mind operates a little differently than what society expects.

Yee Chie Tu is an introverted geek from The Netherlands. Born in Eindhoven and currently based in Bladel. He is mixed blood from a Taiwanese mother and a Singaporean father. He is the tallest in the family with a height of 1.85m.

His interests are baseball, nature, hiking, cycling, anime, gaming, computers, and traveling. Everything he does computer-related is self-taught.

He holds freedom of speech, net neutrality, and privacy in high regard.

The website has been created so he could share his thoughts, opinions, tips, tricks, guides and more. As well to have his place on the massive worldwide web.

Hopefully, you will gain some valuable knowledge from here.

Wonder how to pronounce his name? Head to google translate “e-Chie”.

You only live once.
His way of life these days, for self-improvement and self-acceptance.
