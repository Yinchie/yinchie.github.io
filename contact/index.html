Contact

For any comments or questions, please reach out to me.
Messages sent to me may optionally be signed and or encrypted with my public key.

If you receive an email from me, it will be signed using my secret key.
If it isn’t, assume it has been tampered with or spoofed altogether!

If you are unfamiliar with PGP encryption you can use the website https://apps.crpt.to/encrypt.html, to encrypt your message before sending it to me. It makes use of OnlyKey WebCrypt v2.

WebCrypt is a web application that integrates with OnlyKey and keybase.io to provide secure OpenPGP encryption.
